Privacy

Your data stays yours

No accounts, no tracking, no data collection.

🔒

What leaves your browser

Your chats are read directly from Claude.ai using your active browser session. For ambiguous chat titles, only the title text is sent to a shared AI backend for categorization. No chat content, no personal data, no stored history. The extension has no servers of its own and collects no analytics.

FAQ

Common questions

Is it free?

Yes, completely free. No subscription, no premium tier, no hidden costs.

Do I need an API key?

No. The AI categorization runs on a shared backend. You just install and click Organize.

Will it delete my chats or projects?

No chats are ever deleted. Projects that are left empty after organizing are removed, but all your conversations stay intact and can be found in your chat history.

Does it work in languages other than English?

Yes. The keyword wordlist covers Dutch, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Polish and more. The AI fallback works in any language.

Do I need to be logged in to Claude.ai?

Yes. The extension reads your chats using your active Claude.ai browser session. Open Claude.ai, log in, then click the extension.
